🧃 Why don't juice boxes have soft corners?

Ergonomic study in progress. After bumping into a box falling from the fridge, the author initiated a test protocol based on the pain induced by different packaging. Preliminary results: the lower left corner is the most treacherous.

🪑 Research of March 12, 2023 — Chair vs Stool

Experiment: work 8 hours sitting on a stool, then on a chair.

Results: the back wins, but honor suffers.
Conclusion: the stool is a self-inflicted punishment disguised as furniture.

🦕 The speed of a dinosaur on a scooter (hypothesis)

After several paper simulations, we estimate that a velociraptor equipped with an electric scooter could reach 40 km/h, provided it knows how to use the handlebars.
Next step: find a cooperative velociraptor.

📏 Measuring the universe with an IKEA ruler

Current limit: we are missing 40,075,000 meters of ruler.
